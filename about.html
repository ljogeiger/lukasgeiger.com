About Me

I am currently a junior studying Computer Science at Columbia University (Exp. Grad. Date: May 2021). I am also part of the Lightweight Rowing Team (C150) - see my ROWING page.

My passion for CS and rowing led me to combine the two and build a website. This website is meant to incentivize uploading training data (a key to our team's success) by showing a ranked list of athletes based on hours logged. To create the website we worked with Training Peak's API, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Bootstrap. For more information please visit my PROJECTS page.

I come from Austria but have never lived there and was originally born in Germany. As a third culture kid, I have lived in Switzerland, Japan, Singapore, Ohio, and New York. I do not have one home, rather I view the world as my home scattered with a few special places.
