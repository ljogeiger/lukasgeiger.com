C150 Data

The purpose for this website is to create a motivation for athletes on the Columbia Lightweight Rowing (C150) team to upload their data onto Training Peaks.
Training Peaks is an application the coaches use to monitor fitness and fatigue levels (amongst other data fields).
After logging in, the website displays a ranked list of hours logged taken directly from the Training Peaks API.
