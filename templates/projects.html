C150 Rankings

The purpose for this website is to create a motivation for athletes on the Columbia Rowing (C150) team to upload more of their data onto Training Peaks. Training Peaks is an application the coaches use to monitor fitness and fatigue levels (amongst other data fields). We will display a ranked list of hours logged updated weekly, and, due to the competitive nature between athletes, they will be more motivated to upload all of their data.
